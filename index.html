About Me

I am Daniel, an economist and industrial engineer with a master's in artificial intelligence. I am passionate about connecting people and communities with technological state-of-the-art tools that enhance their quality of life and simplify tasks. Throughout my career, I have gained experience in the public and private sectors, including finances, policies, e-commerce, and market research. My expertise extends to cutting-edge areas such as natural language processing, computer vision, and machine learning, where I have consistently delivered top-notch results.
